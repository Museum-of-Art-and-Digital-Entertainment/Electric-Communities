Group Membership Change

  • View updates local info about group membership

  • Notifies all Dobject presences about group membership change

  • Dobjects *can ask* to create remote presence(s)

    • Root on behalf of all others
    • Each individually
    • Up to designer

  • They can be individually *refused* by remote View (fail TOS)

    • Are notified of failure and reason (exception type)
    • Can do whatever they want/need to do with notification
