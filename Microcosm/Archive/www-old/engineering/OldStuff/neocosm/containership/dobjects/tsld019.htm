View gives facet to DObject (ViewDObjectFacet)

  • DObject gets access to messaging

    • Two interfaces:
      • sendDataToPresences(Data)
      • sendDataToPresencesAtView(viewID, Data)
    • Reliable multicast model
    • Also supports group membership queries: how many, who

  • No other authority provided to Dobject

    • This implements MicroVat
    • By Default: DObject presence completely isolated
