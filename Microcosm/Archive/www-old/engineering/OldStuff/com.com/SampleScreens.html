This sample screen is not a proposal for a specific interface. I created it as a placeholder for completeness of technical design and review.

The ECID interface is completely seperate [near the toolbar] from the ComCom interface. This is because you might want to close ComCom, but keep ECID running (serving up your ID info to your friends....) Also, some of the marketing _scenarios_ do not include ComCom. The ECID interface is acutally a native application that connects to ECID with an ECID interapplication connection.

There are several features missing from these interfaces: ECID needs a way to access the Backlist of People Watching. ComCom doesn't show a Verify User option, doesn't have a Group monitor line, and none of the "lightweight monitors" are shown.
