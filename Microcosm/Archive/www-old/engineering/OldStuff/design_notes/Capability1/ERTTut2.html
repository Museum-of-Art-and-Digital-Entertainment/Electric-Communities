Transferring capability

Only one object can have capability over Receiver at a given time. This means that for Sender2 to access Receiver it is necessary to have Sender1 transfer its exclusive rights over Receiver to Sender2. To do so, Truster1 generates a truster object Truster2for Sender2, transfers the distributor to reach Receiver and last revokes its own distributor to Receiver (see Figure 2). This simple strategy ensures that only one object has rights at a given time.
