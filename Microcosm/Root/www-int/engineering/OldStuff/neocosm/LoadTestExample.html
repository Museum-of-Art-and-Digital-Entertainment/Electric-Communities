A quick copy of a Perl load test script. It essentially issues commands like "Destination moveto" and waits for a reply matching a string. In r167, not all commands produce replies. When functions have two arguments, the second is the string awaited.

I will add documentation upon request.

 

&command("To_Great_Round_Square_West moveto");

expect("automation: Region change completed");

&command("'Here I am on tour $tourCount");

&putat("Ancient_stone_floor");

&pickup($pickupName);

&command("facewest");

&command("perform Bow");

 

&teleportto("Lighthouse", "automation: Region change completed");

&command("'Here I am on tour $tourCount");

&putat("Lighthouse_Deck");

&command("faceeast");

&command("perform Kick");

&pickup($pickupName);

 

&teleportto("The City", "automation: Region change completed");

&putat("Street");

&pickup($pickupName);

&command("'Here I am on tour $tourCount");

&command("perform Dance");

 

&teleportto("Great Round Square", "automation: Region change completed");

&command("'Here I am on tour $tourCount");

&putat("Ancient_stone_floor");

&pickup($pickupName);

&command("perform Shrug");
